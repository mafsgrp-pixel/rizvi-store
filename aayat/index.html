Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom tailoring for abayas?

Yes, Aayat specializes in custom-tailored abayas. Simply provide your measurements during checkout or contact our support team for assistance. Our expert tailors ensure a perfect fit for every customer.

What is the shipping time for international orders?

International shipping typically takes 5-10 business days via DHL/FedEx. Express shipping options (2-4 days) are available at checkout. All orders include tracking and insurance.

Are the fabrics breathable for hot climates?

Absolutely! All Aayat abayas and hijabs are crafted from premium breathable fabrics including Nida, Georgette, and Crepe specifically chosen for comfort in warm climates while maintaining elegant drape.

Do you ship worldwide?

Yes! Aayat ships to over 40 countries worldwide including UAE, UK, USA, Canada, Australia, Saudi Arabia, and across Europe with reliable courier partners and customs expertise.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for standard size items. Custom-tailored pieces are non-returnable but we offer free alterations if needed. Items must be unworn with original tags.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for boutiques and bulk orders. Please contact our wholesale team via WhatsApp for custom quotes and catalog access.
