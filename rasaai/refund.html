💸 Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Cancellation Before Campaign Start

48+ hours before start date: 100% refund of the total campaign cost.
24-48 hours before start date: 50% refund of the total campaign cost.
Less than 24 hours before start date: No refund. Campaign may be rescheduled at no extra cost.

2. Cancellation After Campaign Start

No refund for days already elapsed. For the remaining days, a 50% refund may be provided if cancellation is due to technical issues from RASAAI's side (e.g., LED screen malfunction, audio system failure).

3. Refund Process

Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method (PhonePe, UPI, or bank account). An email confirmation will be sent once the refund is initiated.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The 30 FREE Social Media Creatives bonus has no monetary value and is not refundable. Discounts applied to campaigns are calculated on the final refundable amount.

Please include your campaign ID and invoice number.
