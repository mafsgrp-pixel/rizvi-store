Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when registering: name, email address, phone number, and business details. For drivers, we collect zone and rickshaw information. For passengers, we collect QR scan data to provide rewards.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide and improve our advertising services, process payments, track campaign performance, manage affiliate commissions, and provide customer support.

3. Google Sign-In

When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We do not access your Google Drive, contacts, or any other Google data without explicit permission.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Sheets and is accessible only to you and authorized RASAAI administrators. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to cache data for offline access and improve performance. No tracking cookies are used.
