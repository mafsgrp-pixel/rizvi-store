Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Campaign Cancellation by Client

Before campaign start: 100% refund minus ₹500 processing fee if cancelled 48+ hours before start date.
Within 48 hours: 50% refund.
After campaign start: No refund for days already run. Remaining days refunded at 50%.

2. Campaign Cancellation by RASAAI

If RASAAI cancels a campaign due to technical issues or unavailability, 100% refund is provided including GST.

3. Refund Processing

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method (UPI or Bank Account). A credit note will be issued for all refunds.

4. Non-Refundable Items

GST paid is refundable only if the entire campaign is cancelled before start. Processing fees are non-refundable. Free social media creatives provided have no cash value.

5. Disputes

For refund disputes, contact hello@rasaai.com or WhatsApp +91 95943 06625. We aim to resolve all disputes within 48 hours.
