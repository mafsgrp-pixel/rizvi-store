FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

RASAAI is India's first hyperlocal auto rickshaw advertising network covering 12 zones across Mumbra. We install LED screens and audio systems on rickshaws. Your ads display on these screens and play as audio announcements every 15 minutes during rides, reaching commuters across the city.

Our pricing is dynamic and updates every 15 minutes based on demand and inventory. LED campaigns start at approximately ₹1,238/month per rickshaw, audio campaigns at ₹318/month, and combo packages (LED + Audio) around ₹1,600-1,800/month with up to 18% discount.

We cover all 12 major zones in Mumbra: Kausa, Mumbra Station, Amrut Nagar, Shilphata, Retibunder, Diva Junction, Mumbra Bypass, Check Naka, Kalwa Route, Almas Colony, Azad Nagar, and Mumbra Market. Each zone has 50 rickshaws with detailed population, traffic, and impression analytics.

Every advertiser gets a real-time dashboard showing reach, impressions, CPM, audio plays, GPS routes, and conversion estimates. You can view heatmaps of rickshaw movements, export reports, and monitor performance 24/7.

For LED campaigns: JPG, PNG, WEBP images (recommended 800x400px) or short video loops (MP4, 15-30 seconds). For Audio campaigns: MP3 or WAV files (15-30 seconds). Multi-language audios (Hindi, Marathi, Urdu) are supported for wider reach.

We accept UPI, QR code payments, and manual transaction uploads. Invoices are generated with GST and can be downloaded as PDFs. Payment is required before campaign activation. We also support monthly payment plans for long-term campaigns.
