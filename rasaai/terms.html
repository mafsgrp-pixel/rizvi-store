📋 Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2025

1. Service Description

RASAAI provides outdoor advertising services on auto rickshaws operating across 12 zones in Mumbra, Maharashtra. Services include LED screen advertising, audio announcements via Bluetooth speakers, and campaign analytics.

2. Pricing & Payment

LED Campaigns: ₹1,238 – ₹1,647 per day per rickshaw (dynamic pricing, updates every 15 minutes).
Audio Campaigns: ₹318 – ₹639 per day per rickshaw.
Minimum Booking: 1 rickshaw for 1 day.
Maximum Booking: 50 rickshaws for 90 days.
Discount: 10% off on bookings of 10 or more rickshaws.
Payment Methods: PhonePe, UPI (9594306625@ybl), bank transfer. Payment is required before campaign activation.

3. Campaign Booking & Delivery

Campaigns start on the selected start date. LED screens display ads during rickshaw operating hours. Audio ads play every 15 minutes during rides (zone announcement + 60-second advertisement). Campaign delivery is verified via GPS tracking and playback logs.

4. Cancellation & Refunds

Before Start: Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before start date. 50% refund if cancelled 24-48 hours before.
After Start: No refund for elapsed days. Remaining days may be refunded at 50% if cancelled due to technical issues from RASAAI's side.
Refund Processing: 7-10 business days to original payment method.

5. Creative Content Guidelines

All ad creatives must comply with Indian advertising standards. RASAAI reserves the right to reject content that is offensive, misleading, or violates any laws. LED creatives should be 800x400px (JPG/PNG/WEBP). Audio files should be MP3/WAV format, 15-60 seconds.

6. FREE Social Media Creatives Bonus

First-time clients booking an LED or Combo campaign receive 30 social media creatives FREE. These are ready-to-use designs for Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, Stories, and Carousels. No additional charges apply.

7. Affiliate Program

Affiliates earn 10% commission on bookings made through their referral links. Minimum withdrawal amount is ₹500. Commission is credited after campaign payment is verified.

8. Limitation of Liability

RASAAI strives for 99% ad delivery uptime. However, we are not liable for delays caused by rickshaw breakdowns, traffic restrictions, or force majeure events. In such cases, affected campaign days may be extended or refunded at our discretion.
