🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you register on RASAAI, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and GST number (if applicable). When you book campaigns, we store campaign details including zone selection, creative uploads, and hashtags.

For drivers, we collect GPS location data during duty hours to track rickshaw movements and calculate distance traveled. Audio playback logs are stored for campaign verification.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to: (a) Create and manage your advertising campaigns, (b) Generate invoices and process payments, (c) Provide real-time analytics and campaign performance reports, (d) Track affiliate referrals and calculate commissions, (e) Communicate campaign updates via WhatsApp or email.

3. Data Storage & Security

RASAAI stores data locally in your browser using localStorage for fast, offline-capable access. Campaign data, invoices, and analytics are stored securely. We do not share your personal data with third parties.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your preferences (theme, login session, campaign drafts) for a better user experience. No third-party tracking cookies are used.

5. GPS & Location Data

Driver GPS data is collected only during active duty hours. This data is used to track campaign delivery, calculate distance traveled, and verify ad playback locations. Location data is not shared with advertisers beyond aggregated zone-level reports.

6. Your Rights

You can: (a) Access and update your profile information anytime, (b) Request deletion of your account and associated data, (c) Export your campaign data, (d) Opt out of WhatsApp communications.
