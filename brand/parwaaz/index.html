Frequently Asked Questions

Can I get custom kites with my logo?

Yes, Parwaaz specializes in custom kites with logos, names, and patterns. Perfect for corporate events, festivals, and personal branding.

What is the minimum order for custom kites?

Minimum order for custom printed kites is 100 pieces. For larger events and export orders, we offer bulk discounts up to 40%.

Do you export kites internationally?

Yes, Parwaaz exports kites to 30+ countries worldwide. We handle all export documentation, shipping, and customs clearance.

What materials are used for fighter kites?

Our fighter kites are made from high-quality Japanese tissue paper, bamboo sticks, and glass-coated manja for professional kite fighting.

Do you provide kite strings and accessories?

Yes, we offer complete kite kits including strings, spools, and manja. Custom string colors and lengths available.

What is your lead time for bulk orders?

Standard bulk orders take 15-20 days for production. For urgent orders, express production available with additional charges.
