Frequently Asked Questions

Are your staff background verified?

Yes, all Madadgar staff undergo thorough police verification, reference checks, identity verification, and experience validation before being onboarded.

Do you provide replacement if staff is not suitable?

Yes, we offer free replacement within 15 days if you are not satisfied with the staff. We want to ensure the perfect match for your home.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all major areas of Mumbai including South Mumbai, Bandra, Andheri, Goregaon, Malad, Borivali, Thane, and Navi Mumbai.

What is the minimum hiring period?

The minimum hiring period is 3 months for full-time staff and 1 month for part-time staff. Trial periods are available.

Yes, all our staff receive professional training in housekeeping, cooking, hygiene, and etiquette before placement.

What if my staff takes leave?

We provide replacement staff for any planned or unplanned leaves. Just notify us 24 hours in advance.
