Frequently Asked Questions

Can I customize gifts with photos?

Yes, we offer photo customization on mugs, cushions, photo frames, keychains, and many other products. Simply upload your photo during ordering.

What is the turnaround time for custom gifts?

Standard custom gifts take 3-5 business days for production. Bulk corporate orders may take 7-10 days depending on quantity and complexity.

Do you offer corporate gifting solutions?

Absolutely! We specialize in corporate gifting with custom branding, bulk discounts, premium packaging, and scheduled deliveries.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, Bushra ships to 35+ countries worldwide with tracking. Custom orders may require additional processing time for international shipping.

Can I see a preview before production?

Yes, we provide a digital proof/rendering for approval before we begin production on your custom gift.

What is your return policy for custom gifts?

Custom gifts are non-returnable unless there is a manufacturing defect. We offer free replacements for damaged items.
