The Hidden Risk of Platform Dependency

Relying on third-party marketplaces is convenient — but it comes with long-term business risks that most owners don't see until it's too late.

  • ✦ Price War Exposure: Your product listing appears next to 5-10 competitors. Buyers compare prices instantly. The lowest quote wins — not the best product.
  • ✦ Zero Brand Ownership: Buyers remember IndiaMART, not Mak Enterprise. Repeat orders go through the platform — you don't own the customer relationship.
  • ✦ Algorithm Vulnerability: Platform ranking algorithms change frequently. Your visibility can drop overnight with no warning and no recourse.
  • ✦ Export Blind Spot: International buyers rarely use IndiaMART. They search Google. If you're not on Google, you don't exist to the export market.
