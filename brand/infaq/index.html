Benefits of Donating

Your donation creates a positive impact across multiple dimensions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our donation process

What areas do you currently serve?

We currently operate in Mumbai and Thane. We're planning to expand to other cities soon. Our service areas include all major residential and commercial locations in these cities.

Is there any cost for donation pickup?

No, our doorstep collection service is completely free for donors. We bear all the costs of transportation, sorting, and distribution.

What condition should the donated items be in?

We accept items in usable condition. Clothes should be clean, books should be readable, and household items should be functional. We do minor repairs if needed before distribution.

How do I get the appreciation certificate?

After you submit the donation form, we'll generate a digital certificate immediately. You can download it as PDF or print it directly from your browser.
