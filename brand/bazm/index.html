Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you manage?

Bazm manages all types of events including weddings, engagement parties, corporate events, birthday parties, anniversaries, baby showers, and intimate gatherings.

Do you provide end-to-end event planning?

Yes, we offer complete end-to-end event planning including venue selection, décor, catering, entertainment, photography, and guest management.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Mumbai Metropolitan Region including all major suburbs, but we also plan destination events across India.

How far in advance should I book?

For weddings, we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. For corporate and smaller events, 2-3 months is sufficient.

Do you handle destination weddings?

Yes, we specialize in destination weddings across India including Goa, Udaipur, Jaipur, Kerala, and international locations.

What is your pricing structure?

We offer customized packages based on event size, location, and requirements. Contact us for a free consultation and quote.
