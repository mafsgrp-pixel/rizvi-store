Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer custom furniture design?

Yes, Asaish specializes in custom furniture design. Share your requirements, dimensions, and style preferences, and our master craftsmen will create bespoke pieces tailored to your space.

Do you ship furniture internationally?

Yes, we offer international shipping to 40+ countries with white-glove delivery service including room placement, assembly, and packaging removal.

What materials do you use?

We use premium hardwoods (sheesham, oak, teak, walnut), high-density foam, and luxury fabrics including velvet, linen, and genuine leather.

What is the delivery time for custom orders?

Custom furniture typically takes 4-6 weeks for completion, including design approval and handcrafting. Ready-to-ship items deliver in 7-10 days.

Do you offer warranty?

Yes, all Asaish furniture comes with a 5-year warranty on the frame and 2-year warranty on upholstery and cushions.

Can I see fabric samples before ordering?

Absolutely! We offer free fabric and wood sample swatches delivered to your home so you can feel the quality before ordering.
