Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver flowers internationally?

Yes, Sanobar delivers fresh and preserved flowers to 40+ countries worldwide with guaranteed freshness, careful packaging, and real-time tracking.

How long do preserved flowers last?

Our preserved flowers last 1-3 years without water or sunlight. They require zero maintenance and stay beautiful forever - no watering, no wilting.

Do you offer custom arrangements for weddings?

Absolutely! We specialize in wedding floral arrangements including bridal bouquets, centerpieces, mandap decoration, and venue flowers.

Can I add a personalized message?

Yes, every order includes a free handwritten card with your personalized message. You can also add photo prints or small gifts.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

Same-day delivery is available in Mumbai for orders placed before 2 PM. International orders require 2-5 days for delivery.

Do you have corporate gifting options?

Yes, we offer corporate gifting solutions with bulk discounts, custom branding, and scheduled deliveries for all occasions.
