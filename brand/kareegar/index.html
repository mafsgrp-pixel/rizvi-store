Welcome to Kareegar — where metal transforms into art. We specialize in custom metal fabrication for homes and offices including decorative gates, window grills, staircase railings, and security solutions. Every piece is crafted with precision, finished to perfection, and installed professionally. With Kareegar, your space gets the perfect blend of strength and beauty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you work with?

We specialize in wrought iron, mild steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and brass for all types of metal fabrication projects.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes, we offer completely custom designs. Share your ideas, photos, or sketches, and our craftsmen will bring your vision to life.

Do you provide installation?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our products across Mumbai with a warranty on workmanship.

What is the typical turnaround time?

Standard orders take 7-10 days for fabrication and installation. Custom designs may take 2-3 weeks depending on complexity.

Do you offer powder coating?

Yes, we offer powder coating, spray painting, and anti-rust treatment for long-lasting durability.

What is your warranty?

We offer a 2-year warranty on all fabrication work and a 5-year warranty on powder coating/paint.
